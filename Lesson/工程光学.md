## 题解：
### 1. 计算光线进入第一个透镜：

![[例1.svg]]
**此处只给出第一步的光路图，之后的光路图同理**

$$l_{1}=-\infty,u_{1}=0,r_{1}=26.67mm,n_1=1,n_{1}'=1.614,h_{1}=10mm$$
$$i_{1}=\frac{h_{1}}{r_{1}}=\frac{10}{26.67}=0.374953$$
$$i_{1}'=\frac{n}{n'}i_{1}=\frac{1}{1.614}\times \frac{10}{26.67}=0.232313$$
$$u_{1}'=u_{1}+i_{1}-i_{1}'=0+0.374953-0.232313=0.14264$$
$$\frac{n'}{l_{1}'}-\frac{n}{l_{1}}=\frac{n'-n}{r_{1}}~~\Rightarrow~~ \frac{1.614}{l_{1}'}-0=\frac{0.614}{26.67}~~\Rightarrow~~ l_{1}'=70.106482mm$$
### 2.光线射出第一个透镜：
$$u_{2}=u_{1}'=0.14264,r_{2}=189.67mm,n_{2}=1.614,n_{2}'=1,d_{2}=5.2mm$$
$$l_{2}=l_{1}'-d_{2}=70.106482-5.2=64.906482mm$$
$$h_{2}=l_{2}\times u_{2}=64.906482\times 0.14264=9.258260mm$$
$$\frac{n_{2}'}{l_{2}'}-\frac{n_{2}}{l_{2}}=\frac{n_{2}'-n_{2}}{r_{2}}~~\Rightarrow~~ \frac{1}{l_{2}'}-\frac{1.614}{64.906482}=-\frac{0.614}{189.67}~~\Rightarrow~~ l_{2}'=46.233487mm$$
$$u_{2}'=\frac{h_{2}}{l_{2}'}=\frac{9.258260}{46.233487}=0.200250$$
### 3.光线射入第二个透镜：
$$u_{3}=u_{2}'=0.200250,r_{3}=-49.66mm,n_{3}=1,n_{3}'=1.6475,d_{3}=7.95mm$$
$$l_{3}=l_{2}'-d_{3}=46.233487-7.95=38.283487mm$$
$$h_{3}=l_{3}\times u_{3}=38.283487\times 0.200250=7.666268mm$$
$$\frac{n_{3}'}{l_{3}'}-\frac{n_{3}}{l_{3}}=\frac{n_{3}'-n_{3}}{r_{3}}\Rightarrow \frac{1.6475}{l_{3}'}-\frac{1}{38.283487}=\frac{0.6475}{-49.66}~~\Rightarrow~~ l_{3}'=125.933901mm$$
$$u_{3}'=\frac{h_{3}}{l_{3}'}=\frac{7.666268}{125.933901}=0.060875$$
### 4.光线射出第二个透镜
$$u_{4}=u_{3}'=0.060875,r_{4}=25.47mm,n_{4}=1.6475,n_{4}'=1,d_{4}=1.6mm$$
$$l_{4}=l_{3}'-d_{4}=125.933901-1.6=124.333901mm$$
$$h_{4}=l_{4}\times u_{4}=124.333901\times 0.060875=7.568826mm$$
$$\frac{n_{4}'}{l_{4}'}-\frac{n_{4}}{l_{4}}=\frac{n_{4}'-n_{4}}{r_{4}}\Rightarrow \frac{1}{l_{4}'}-1.$$
